About Us

Craving some delicious Greek food? Maybe you’re in the mood for a juicy steak? No matter what kind of meal you have in mind, The Food Corner is ready to prepare it for you.

Since 2000, The Food Corner has been the go-to diner for residents of Dhaka, BD. Our diner serves breakfast all day, in addition to wholesome and flavorful dining options for lunch and dinner. From burgers to salads, seafood to pastas, you’ll find all kinds of hearty meals prepared fresh at The Food Corner. Our diner also has a full bakery with delicious baked goods and other treats, including our famous cheesecake. Sounds delicious, right?
